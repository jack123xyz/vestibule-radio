## Claiming an area

1. Open a PR replacing `_unclaimed_` with your GitHub username
2. Mention it in ` ` so people know
3. That's it. You're the point person now. Doesn't mean you work alone, just that you're steering.

Multiple people can co-own an area. List everyone separated by commas. Encouraged for bigger components like the frontend or streaming stack.
